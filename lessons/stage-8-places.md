# Stage 8: Places & Daily Life

## Grammar Notes

- **Location with Ở**: Subject + ở + [place] → "Tôi ở thành phố" (I am in the city / I live in the city)
- **Distance with Cách**: A + cách + B + [number + unit] → "Nhà tôi cách đây 5km" (My house is 5km from here)
- **Gần** (near) follows the verb or place: "Nhà tôi gần đây" (My house is near here)
- **Đó** (there) points to something mentioned or visible: "ở đó" (over there)
- **Về thăm** is a fixed phrase: to return home for a visit
