# Stage 6: Descriptive Vocabulary

## Grammar Notes

- **Adjectives follow the noun**: "hoa đỏ" (red flower), "bầu trời xanh" (blue sky)
- As a predicate, no "là": "Bạn đẹp" (You are beautiful), "Trời nóng" (It is hot)
- **Weather**: "Trời + [adj]" → Trời nóng; "Có + [noun]" → Có nắng; "Nhiều + [noun]" → Nhiều mây
- **Emotions**: Subject + [emotion], no verb needed: "Tôi hạnh phúc"
- **Colors with nouns**: "Hoa màu đỏ" or "Hoa đỏ" — both correct

### Tier 3 — Complex descriptive sentence

| English | Vietnamese |
|---|---|
| Yesterday Hanoi's autumn was very beautiful | Ngày hôm qua mùa thu Hà Nội rất đẹp |
